Where It All Began

The Sonic Foundation

Trained ear. Emotional intelligence. Two decades at the sharp end of music and film production.

I've worked in rooms with Hans Zimmer and Trevor Horn — not as an observer, but as part of the team making it happen. Those environments set a standard I've never let go of.

A period running large-scale IT infrastructure projects taught me something unexpected: the best productions, like the best systems, succeed because of rigorous process and calm under pressure. I brought that back to audio.

Now I'm applying everything — the ear, the instinct, the precision — to radio, podcast, and broadcast production. Because great audio isn't just technically clean. It has rhythm. It has theatre — the theatre of the mind. It makes the listener feel something.

The theatre of the mind.
